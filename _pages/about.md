I'm an M.S. candidate in Artificial Intelligence (Sep 2023 – Jul 2026) at [Zhengzhou University](https://english.zzu.edu.cn/). My research interests include Text-to-SQL, LLM, VLM, Reinforcement Learning, reasoning, Argument Mining, Text Classification, and Knowledge Graph–based Question Answering.

I am advised by Prof. Hongying Zan at the Natural Language Processing Laboratory, Zhengzhou University. Previously, I received my B.Eng. in Software Engineering (Sep 2018 – Jul 2022) from [Chengdu University of Information Technology](https://english.cuit.edu.cn/).
